About Us

At Pradech 360, We Manage, Develop and Consult with talent at all levels and in all fields of the entertainment industry.

Established in 2022, Pradech 360 is a company founded by creatives for creatives, with a mission to help creatives maneuver through the troubles faced in the creative industry. It is a full service creative agency connecting creatives and brands, working together with creatives in different areas including management, marketing, publicity, brand development, artiste and repertoire, event production and management

As a full service company we use a hybrid of traditional and modern strategies to establish and develop the careers of the creative we work with. Our business model enables you to keep all your rights and publishing while we work to amplify what you create.

Helping creative people achieve their goals and make a living from their craft by providing effective, modern and timely solutions. At Pradech360, we’re regular communicators and narrators; Together we offer custom fitted solutions that make our clients messages remarkable. We are not just experts but partners and accomplices pushing you further towards your goal.

We equip you with the tools you need to stay original while performing with quality at the highest levels.
